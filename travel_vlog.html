Travel Tips

Helpful advice and tips for making your travel experiences...

  • Pack Light: Only carry essentials and versatile clothing to avoid overpacking.
  • Carry a Reusable Water Bottle: One will not forget to keep him or herself hydrated while also saving the plastic.
  • Have a Digital Copy of Important Documents: Take pictures or scan your passport, tickets, and travel insurance
  • Make Use of Local Public Transport: It is usually cheaper; it gives one the feel of the local way of life.
  • Get Travel Insurance: You never know what may happen when you are traveling; medical emergencies can strike or luggage can get lost.
  • Learn a few key phrases in local language: These may include word of welcome, basic greetings and more to start.
  • Let your bank know you are traveling: So your credit card doesn't get put on hold due to international transactions
  • Pack a portable first aid kit: Include band aids, pain relievers, and any prescription medications you might take.
  • Dress like the locals: Avoid standing out in a crowd as a tourist by dressing like everyone else.
  • Avoid being a victim of local scams: Understand common scams in that area to avoid falling into their traps.
